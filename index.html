About Farm

Who we are

Fresh Station Eco Farm is a lifetime project of Jane and Will Barso, who started it as an exemplary way of living in harmony with the environment and eating healthy. Today, we are happy to cherish the full-functioning farm that was once a 20-acres amateur project, providing the customers with fresh products and teaching the ways to be more friendly to our planet.

Our products

Over the decades we established and improved the technology of growing and making organic products. Now, we can offer far more than just garden-grown products. We collect honey, make cheese, breed chickens, and cows. In addition to the fresh fruit and vegetables from our garden, we make jam, syrups, and bread. Check updates for the seasonal products in our online shop.

Why organic?

In the 1970s, Jane and Will, inspired by the unraveling movement towards environmental sustainability and ecological mindfulness, strove to design a way of doing less damage to the planet and bring harmony between the food production and nature. That harmony manifests a healthy life and work attitude. Today our organic business is popular, and we are glad to offer our products and promote biodiversity as a better lifestyle.
